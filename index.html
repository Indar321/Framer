ORIGIN PROTOCOL

Initialized by Indrajeet Pisda, Virox operates as a reaction against mass-produced, flimsy fashion. Sourced from the core concepts of digital brutalism and architectural clothing, we view garments not as mere covering, but as hardware.

The mission is strictly defined: Create heavy, structured, unyielding pieces that hold their shape and make a definitive statement.

MATERIAL SPECS

A silhouette is only as strong as its foundation. We refuse lightweight fabrics. Our t-shirts utilize a minimum of 280gsm cotton for a rigid, boxy drape. Outerwear is constructed from high-tensile nylons, weatherproof ripstop, and heavy thermal linings.

OPERATIONAL LOOP

PHASE A: DRAFT

Silhouettes are engineered digitally. Shoulders are dropped precisely. Cropped lengths are calculated.

PHASE B: PROCUREMENT

We hunt for the densest, most durable fabrics available on the global market to ensure standards are met.

PHASE C: EXECUTION

Heavy-duty hardware insertion. Reinforced stitching across all tension points. Rigorous QA.

RETURNS POLICY

14-DAY PROTOCOL

We accept returns and exchanges within 14 days of delivery. To be eligible, your item must be unworn, unwashed, and in the exact condition that you received it.

THE PROCESS

To initiate a return, please contact our support team with your order number. A flat fee will be deducted from your refund to cover return shipping costs.

EXCEPTIONS

Limited edition drop items and accessories (beanies, bags) are final sale and cannot be returned unless defective.
