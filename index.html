CORE

01. ORIGIN PROTOCOL

Initialized by Indrajeet Pisda, Virox operates as a reaction against mass-produced, flimsy fashion. Sourced from the core concepts of digital brutalism and architectural clothing, we view garments not as mere covering, but as hardware.

The mission is strictly defined: Create heavy, structured, unyielding pieces that hold their shape and make a definitive statement. The palette is intentionally restricted to black and white to emphasize form over distraction.

02. MATERIAL SPECS

A silhouette is only as strong as its foundation. We refuse lightweight fabrics. Our t-shirts utilize a minimum of 280gsm cotton for a rigid, boxy drape. Outerwear is constructed from high-tensile nylons, weatherproof ripstop, and heavy thermal linings.

03. OPERATIONAL LOOP

PHASE A: DRAFT

Silhouettes are engineered digitally. Shoulders are dropped precisely. Cropped lengths are calculated to layer flawlessly with core pieces.

PHASE B: PROCUREMENT

We hunt for the densest, most durable fabrics available on the global market to ensure our extreme structural standards are met.

PHASE C: EXECUTION

Heavy-duty hardware insertion. Reinforced stitching across all tension points. Rigorous QA. The asset is then cleared for drop.

INTEL / FAQ

Before opening a comms channel, review the system parameters below.

Our signature fit is inherently boxy, slightly cropped, with pronounced dropped shoulders. Order your true size to achieve the intended architectural look. Sizing down will result in a more standard fit.

We operate on a strict drop model. Once a collection is released, inventory is limited. When an item sells out, it is archived permanently. No restocks. Subscribe to the network ping for alerts on new drops.

We offer standard dispatch across India within 3-5 business days from our Dantewada HQ. Global dispatch is active; international rates and delivery estimates are calculated dynamically at checkout.

To maintain structural integrity: Machine wash cold, inside out. Use mild detergent. Do not bleach. Hang dry in a shaded area. Do not tumble dry. Never iron directly over screen prints or hardware.

LEGAL DATA

Data collection, returns protocols, and terms of use are governed strictly by the Virox Systems mandate. Review thoroughly.

1. RETURNS POLICY

We accept returns and exchanges within 14 days of receipt. Assets must remain unworn, unwashed, and retain all original structural tags. Limited drop items are considered final sale and cannot be returned unless a manufacturing defect is verified.

2. DATA USAGE (PRIVACY)

We collect necessary telemetry to process orders and optimize grid performance. Financial information provided during secure checkout is fully encrypted via industry-standard protocols and never stored on our local servers.
