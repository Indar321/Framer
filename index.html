THE DIRECTIVE

01. STRUCTURAL INTEGRITY

We reject lightweight materials. Every garment is constructed using hyper-dense fabrics ranging from 280gsm cotton to 500gsm fleece. The silhouette must hold its shape independent of the wearer.

02. MONOCHROMATIC PURITY

Color is a distraction. The Virox system relies purely on stark contrast, extreme textures, and meticulous pattern making to convey aesthetic dominance.

ORIGIN PROTOCOL

Initialized by Indrajeet Pisda, Virox operates as a reaction against mass-produced, flimsy fashion. Sourced from the core concepts of digital brutalism and architectural clothing, we view garments not as mere covering, but as hardware.
