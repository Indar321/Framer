Knowledge Base

Standard operating procedures and frequent queries.

What is the standard turnaround time? +

Brand identities and logo systems typically require 1-2 weeks. Full-scale web platforms and e-commerce builds take between 3-6 weeks depending on architectural complexity.

Do you provide source files? +

Yes. We don't hold brands hostage. Upon final payment, all intellectual property rights and raw source files (Figma, Illustrator, complete codebases) are securely transferred to your servers.

What platforms do you build on? +

We engineer custom front-end solutions using modern web standards (HTML5/CSS3/JS). For e-commerce, we specialize in Shopify. For simpler content sites, we utilize platforms like Blogger and Google Sites based on client budget.

How does communication work during a project? +

Direct and efficient. We primarily utilize our secure WhatsApp line for quick updates, and formal email transmissions for asset delivery and milestone approvals.

Do you manage hosting and domains? +

We facilitate the initial setup, DNS configuration, and connection. However, hosting and domain registrar accounts are created entirely under your name to ensure you retain total legal ownership.

How do revision rounds work? +

Most standard deployment tiers include 2 dedicated rounds of revision per milestone. A 'round' constitutes a consolidated list of feedback. Further revisions are billed on a modular basis.

Do you offer post-launch maintenance? +

Yes. Clients can engage our studio via our Enterprise Retainer for ongoing updates, graphic support, and SLA-backed maintenance, or request ad-hoc updates via our A La Carte menu.
