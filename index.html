THE CORE

ORIGIN

Founded by Indrajeet Pisda, Virox emerged from the creative and analytical ecosystem of digital design and brand building. Recognizing a void in the market for truly structural, heavyweight everyday apparel, the system was initialized.

We view garments as hardware. They must function, they must endure, and they must carry an unmistakable aesthetic presence.

MATERIALS

We do not compromise on textiles. Our standard tees utilize 280gsm cotton, providing a heavy drape that holds its boxy shape. Outerwear pieces utilize water-resistant nylons and dense ripstop to withstand harsh conditions.

OPERATIONAL METHOD

01. DRAFT

Silhouettes are engineered digitally, calculating drop shoulders and crop lengths before a physical sample is ever cut.

02. SECURE

Procuring dense, premium fabrics globally to ensure extreme structural integrity and longevity.

03. EXECUTE

Reinforced stitching, custom acid wash treatments, and rigorous QA testing prior to release.

INTEL / FAQ

Before opening a comms channel, review our operational parameters below.

Our signature fit is inherently boxy, slightly cropped, with pronounced dropped shoulders. We recommend ordering your true size to achieve the intended architectural look. Sizing down will result in a more standard fit.

We operate on a strict drop model. Once a collection is released, inventory is limited. When an item sells out, it is archived permanently. Subscribe to the network ping for alerts on new drops.

We offer standard dispatch across India within 3-5 business days. Global dispatch is available; international rates and delivery estimates are calculated dynamically at checkout.

To maintain structural integrity: Machine wash cold, inside out. Use mild detergent. Do not bleach. Hang dry in shaded area. Do not tumble dry. Never iron directly over screen prints or hardware.

LEGAL DATA

Data collection, returns protocols, and terms of use are governed strictly by the Virox Systems mandate. Review thoroughly before proceeding.

1. RETURNS POLICY

We accept returns and exchanges within 14 days of receipt. Assets must remain unworn, unwashed, and retain all original structural tags. Limited drop items are considered final sale and cannot be returned unless a manufacturing defect is verified.

2. DATA USAGE (PRIVACY)

We collect necessary telemetry to process orders and optimize grid performance. Financial information provided during secure checkout is fully encrypted and never stored on local servers.
